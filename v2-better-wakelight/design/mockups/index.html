Wakelight — UI direction mockups

Ten directions for the rebuilt V2 firmware UI. The same scenario throughout — wake 07:00, Mon–Fri, 30-minute sunrise, lamp named Bedroom — so you can compare apples to apples. Most controls are live (toggles, sliders, day pills, dials, tabs). Open each full-screen, click around, and tell me what works and what doesn't. They explore different aesthetics and different interaction models for the three core jobs: set the light · set a recurring wake · turn on/off.

Tip: open any mockup full-screen and resize your browser narrow to feel the phone layout. Advanced features (effects, DMX address, fixture profile, second alarm) are intentionally tucked away in most of these.
